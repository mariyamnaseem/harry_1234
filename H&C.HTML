Humanitarian And Compassionate Grounds

A Humanitarian and Compassionate (H&C) application in Canada is a request for permanent residence on humanitarian and compassionate grounds. It's typically submitted by individuals who are unable to meet the regular requirements for permanent residency but have compelling reasons to stay in Canada. These reasons could include factors such as establishment in Canada, best interests of any children involved, and Best Immigration and Visa Consultant in Canada & Ludhiana for H&C Application the potential for hardship if returned to their home country. H&C applications must include comprehensive supporting documents as well as an engaging story that explains the applicant's exceptional situation and justifications for seeking special attention. In the event that the application is approved, they might be given humanitarian and compassionate grounds for permanent residence, which would enable them to stay in Canada and provide for their families.

Eligibility
